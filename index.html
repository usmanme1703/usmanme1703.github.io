02. THE TRANSFORMATION

My journey didn't start with code; it started with community. My mindset was shaped early on by the discipline and service ingrained in me by the Scout movement, MSSN, Interact (Rotary Club), and the Nigeria Red Cross. Volunteering with organizations like WARIF and Debra Palace Initiative taught me that true impact requires empathy, but to scale that impact, I needed to master business and technology.

The strategic spark ignited in August 2024 at the Youth Entrepreneurship and Leadership Academy (YELA). Through rigorous case studies, I learned to architect and pitch viable business models. Soon after, the Be-MINT program transformed me into an active builder, pivoting me into digital prototyping. This momentum led to specialized CAD training with Siemens, where I mastered Solid Edge to bring mechanical concepts to reality.

Armed with a multidisciplinary toolkit, I dove into the industry. I joined Team VOC AI to a 1st Place victory at Wema Bank's Hackaholics 6.0, joined JOVID Engineering Solutions as a Design Engineering Intern, and drove impact as a CEAP Ambassador & Product Manager also the Social Media Manager at Cybernovr. My commitment to this path was honored when I became a Debra Palace Initiative (DPI) Scholar.

Today, as a Teenage Mechatronics Engineering student, my mission is clear:
Bridging hardware precision with software scalability through Entrepreneurship.
